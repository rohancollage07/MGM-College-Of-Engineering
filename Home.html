About the Event

IBSSC-2022 will provide a forum that will bring together researchers, academia and practitioners from industry to meet and exchange their ideas and recent research achievements in all aspects of healthcare, agriculture, intelligent transportation, and renewable electronics, together with their applications in the contemporary world. Themed, “Frontiers of Technologies: Fueling Prosperity of the Planet and People,” this flagship conference of the IEEE Bombay.

Call For Papers

The 4th IEEE Bombay Section Signature Conference (IBSSC-2022), is an international conference organized by IEEE Bombay Section and SVKM’s NMIMS MPSTME, Mumbai, India. This flagship event will serve as a forum for disseminating the most recent advances in AI, Data Science, ML, and Robotics & Automation, as well as potential research prospects. The Magical City, Mumbai will be the host for the IBSSC-2022.
